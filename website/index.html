Case Study

Results obtained in the Women's Fashion sector

A large fashion retailer faced a critical dilemma: chronic overstock in some regions and stockouts in others. Since partnering with Shift, they've transformed their inventory planning.

Challenge

  • Millions in working capital immobilized in slow-moving stock.
  • Manual planning took months to assemble the new collection.
  • Replenishment far exceeded real demand in several stores.

Solution

  • Developed a demand forecasting model at the SKU-Store level.
  • Automated allocation based on real-time sales probability.
  • Implemented a replenishment system that adapts to trends.
