Success Story

Case Study: Fashion Retail

A leading fashion retailer faced a critical dilemma: chronic overstock in some regions and stockouts in others. They turned to Shift to transform their inventory planning.

The Challenge

  • Millions in working capital tied up in slow-moving inventory.
  • Manual planning couldn't handle sporadic demand for low-volume products.
  • Stocking multiple times more than actual demand across all stores.

The Solution

  • Implemented probabilistic forecasting to quantify demand uncertainty.
  • Optimized inventory levels per store/SKU based on service level targets.
  • Deployed real-time dashboards for continuous monitoring.
